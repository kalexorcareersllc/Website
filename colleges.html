Why partner with Kalexor?

  • Absolutely free — no subscription, no cost to your career center.
  • Hassle-free — we manage candidate intake, screenings, and communications.
  • Human element prioritized — personality and cultural fit are core to our matching.

  • All opportunities verified — we vet employers and roles before sharing.
  • Zero risk — if a placement doesn't work, we quickly place the candidate elsewhere.
  • Placement support — designed to help you increase your college's placement rate.

1 — Partnership

2 — Match
